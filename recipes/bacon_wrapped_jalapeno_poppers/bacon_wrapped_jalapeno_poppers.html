Bacon-Wrapped Jalapeno Poppers

Like all great sports, two forces are coming together to make magic happen! For today's match, savory and spicy come together to make Bacon-Wrapped Jalapeño Poppers! With bubbling cheese, savory bacon and spicy peppers, these bacon wrapped jalapenos will have every guest reaching for another.

Ingredients

  • ½ cup cream cheese
  • ½ cup shredded sharp Cheddar cheese
  • 12 jalapeno peppers, halved lengthwise, seeds and membranes removed
  • 12 slices bacon

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C). Line a baking sheet with aluminum foil.
  2. Mix cream cheese and Cheddar cheese together in a bowl until evenly blended. Fill each jalapeno half with the cheese mixture. Put halves back together and wrap each stuffed pepper with a slice of bacon. Arrange bacon-wrapped peppers on the prepared baking sheet.
  3. Bake in the preheated oven until bacon is crispy, about 15 minutes.
